About Saint Francois Xavier College-Apebu Nyamata TVET School

This school has been initiated and started by a grouped parents in 1986 with the common objective of Promoting the level of Education in Bugesera district. The school has boarding students and day scholars.

values

-Excellence -Passion -Hardworking -Patriotism -Dignity -Professionalism -Creativeness -Innovativeness.

MISSION

To make Rwandan youth competent in technical skills by implementing Rwandan TVET programs and establishment of adequate, efficient and appropriate TVET offers in Rwanda.

Vision

To be a leading TVET school offers quality and competence in Rwanda.
